Why do I hate taking photos?
Pictures
In this "article" I will try to explain why I consider it strange to
take photos.
For what reason?
Most of the time I say to people who do not like taking pictures they end up amazed, then I ask, why do you take photos? for the most part they respond that "it is to keep good times", but if these moments are really good because the need to have a photo to remind them? Theoretically these moments would be fixed in the memory.
And I'm not photogenic either.
